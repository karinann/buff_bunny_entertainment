# buff_bunny_entertainment

**Introduction**
An AI-driven academic “manager” that helps college students track degree requirements, course offerings, and schedules to hit specific goals like internships, scholarship, or skill building through the lens of a Pop Stardom. By analyzing onboarding data and chat histories, the program offers personalized suggestions to users and an interactive dashboard, using a pop-culture inspired concept to motivate students to reach for the stars.

Target Group:
UCF Undergraduate Students between the age of 18 and 25. Their personalities are ambitious, and goal driven. Undergraduate students want a way to simulate their decisions that affect their, schedule, personal time, and goals without having to spend hours manually doing it themselves.

Primary User:
UCF undergraduate students juggling heavy schedules trying to build towards a career.

**Problem**
- Internal Tools (Pegasus Path, Degree Audit, Custom Spreadsheets): While these tools allow students to simulate paths, they require manual input of data and cross reference amongst several tools for an accurate read. Some counselors even warn students that Pegasus Path may be inaccurate.
- Human Services (UCF Academic & Career Services): While these services help students develop a plan towards career-goals, they rely entirely on student-initiated outreach and lack personalization with thousands of students to manage. T
- Flaws: Existing solutions push 100% of the administrative burden to exhausted students wasting time away from progress towards their career path and goals. They need the ability to be able to always receive a quick answer to lessen the pressure they feel which in turn will motivate students progress even more towards goals.

**Our Solution**
- A personal “manager” available to students 24/7 to keep track of progress towards career goals (internship, degree requirement, coursework, skill building, etc.) and to answer students’ question opening more time for students to work towards the goals they set.
- Students will answer a pre-survey and upload degree requirements and supporting documents (ex. Course offerings, elective list, etc.) to our AI to receive a curated schedule and suggested goals for the student to follow on their dashboard. At any point, students can add onto their schedule and goals by speaking with the AI and receive answers for any of their questions. 

MVP Scope
- AI Chatbot + Chat History Database
- Pre-set Database (ex. Name, Year, Completed Credits, Total Credits etc.)
- Figma Demo
    * Onboarding Survey
    * Dashboard (Schedule, Goals, etc.)
    * Manager Chat Screen
    * User Pop-Star Customization
